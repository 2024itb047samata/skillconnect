# SkillConnect – Digital Labour Marketplace

## Overview

SkillConnect is a web platform that connects customers with skilled workers such as plumbers, electricians, painters, masons, carpenters, mechanics, and other service professionals.

The platform helps customers quickly find reliable workers while creating more employment opportunities for skilled labourers through a digital marketplace.

---

## Problem Statement

Finding skilled workers often requires visiting labour markets physically or relying on word-of-mouth recommendations. This process is inefficient, time-consuming, and limits opportunities for workers.

---

## Solution

SkillConnect provides a digital platform where:

- Customers can search and hire skilled workers.
- Workers can showcase their skills and find jobs.
- Users can book services and track job status.
- Ratings and reviews improve trust and transparency.
- Verified worker profiles increase reliability.
